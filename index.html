ERIS GALAXY

Is the first game in a trilogy of free to play and invest to earn games that will open the doors to a metaverse, which will challenge its players by encouraging them to team up, organize, build and fight their way into unknown territories.

The History

With the arrival of order, the CHAOS had to balance the scales of destiny, so it was decided from the beginning. A prophecy spoke that the chaos, which was once enclosed, was going to resurface more powerful than ever, destroying everything in its path. That is why now these races: Humans, Clasumas, Streps, Flowlings and Ruts, were threatened by such monstrosity, and were forced to join forces to bring order back to the galaxy.

GamePlay

ALL VS GRIMER

A simple battle mode, where the six race users must kill the Grimer and make sure the ship does not lose its functionalities. This mode will be the easiest for races, and more dynamic for those who play with the Grimer.

From the point of view of the races, it is a game mode adapted to have fun and have a good time without having to be so alert unlike other modes.

This mode can only be played as a “normal game”.

WATCH

ALL VS ALL

This game mode is a challenge for everyone, they must know how to use their resources and skills well, be prudent with their movements, it is not a mode for newbies, entering here is for those people who want to show what they are made of.

This mode can be played both as a “normal game” and as a “ranked game”.
